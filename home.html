YOUR HEALTH MATTERS !

PURPOSE:

The CLT HEALTH HELP Team wants to Provide free healthcare resources for Charlotte residents, To Provide advocacy resources to obtain universal healthcare in the United States, To provide adequate financial support to individuals and families who are unable to afford healthcare and to raise advocacy for a fair and equitable national healthcare system.

STATISTICS:

Many individuals and families in the Charlotte area are unable to pay large financial healthcare cost which may cause: Families not being able to seek medical attention or seeking medical attention too late, Cutting back on spending on food, clothing, or basic household items, Used up all or most of savings, Taken an extra job or worked more hours.
